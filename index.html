Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use this?

We believe that Talos News makes reading news efficent and optimizes ones ability to gather information. One can be fully informed on any topic by reading a small memo roughly the size of a few tweets.

How does it work?

Talos News aggregates news from hundreds of sources and then pumpes it into an advanced text summarization algorithm that turns that given topic into a daily memo.

How much is it?

It is completely free for daily news. Though it is five dollars a month for personalized feeds and our live breaking news section.
